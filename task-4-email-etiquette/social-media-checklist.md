# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do’s

* Think before you post – ensure content is appropriate and respectful
* Use professional language and correct grammar
* Respect others’ opinions and engage politely
* Protect your privacy and personal information
* Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading misinformation)

## ❌ Don’ts

* Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
* Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information
* Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
* Avoid oversharing personal or sensitive details
* Do not use social media irresponsibly during academic or professional activities
