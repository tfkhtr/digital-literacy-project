# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Think before you click: Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media.
- Use strong passwords: Create unique passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
- Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords, ID details) with anyone online.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into accounts.
- Keep your apps and devices updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Download apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

## Financial / UPI Safety
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only required for sending money.
- Do not approve unknown “Collect Requests” on UPI apps.
- Double-check payment requests and QR codes before making any transaction.
- Avoid sharing screenshots of transactions that contain sensitive details.

## Social Media Awareness
- Keep your profiles private and limit personal information visibility.
- Do not accept friend requests from unknown people.

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime:
- Report immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**

## Conclusion
Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself online. A few careful steps can prevent major cyber threats.
